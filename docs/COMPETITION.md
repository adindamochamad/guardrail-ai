## 🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### From Rules & Past Analysis

❌ **Late submission**
- Deadline is HARD: 10:00 AM PDT May 28
- NO extensions accepted
- Submit at least 6 hours early

❌ **Incomplete "The Whole Story"**
- Missing business model → low feasibility score
- Missing challenges section → looks too easy
- Missing "what's next" → no vision

❌ **Poor demo video**
- Too long (> 3 minutes) → judges skip
- No live demo → low progress score
- Reading slides → boring
- Bad audio → unwatchable

❌ **Generic problem statement**
- "Many developers have problems" → too vague
- Better: "70% of developers ship AI code without review"

❌ **Not selecting sponsor challenges**
- Leave money on the table
- Satu project BISA submit ke BANYAK challenges

❌ **Mockup instead of working demo**
- Progress score akan sangat rendah
- Judge ingin lihat REAL code running
